# ազդ | azd

**A bilingual art & activism exhibition centering women's voices and experiences in public spaces.**

---

## 📖 About the Project

**ազդ (azd)** is a digital exhibition exploring women's presence as an act of intervention and redefinition of public space in Gyumri. The project centers on how women's voices are often silenced or confined to private spheres, and how reclaiming urban space becomes a form of resistance and visibility.

### Core Concept
In Gyumri, public space has been shaped predominantly by male presence and gaze. This project reclaims that space through women, positioning them not only as visible but as active agents of transformation. It addresses the fundamental question: **How can women's presence become irreversible?**
